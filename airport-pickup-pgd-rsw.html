Landing at PGD or RSW? Your Guide to Easy Airport Car Pickup

Southwest Florida has two convenient gateways — Punta Gorda Airport (PGD) and Fort Myers / Southwest Florida International (RSW). Renting through Turo means you can skip the rental-counter line and have a clean, ready-to-go car waiting when you land. Here's how it works with SafeWheels.

PGD vs. RSW

PGD is small, fast, and easy — popular with Allegiant travelers, with quick curbside meetups. RSW is the larger international airport with more flight options. We serve both, so book whichever fits your flight.

How pickup works

  • Book your dates and vehicle on our Turo profile.
  • Choose airport pickup at PGD or RSW (or delivery within 50 miles of 33955).
  • We coordinate a meeting spot through the Turo app and hand off the keys after a quick walk-around.
  • Verify your identity and add any extra drivers in Turo before your trip.

Why it beats the counter

No long lines, no hard-sell upgrades, and transparent pricing. You deal directly with a local Turo All-Star Host who keeps the fleet meticulously maintained — and who actually knows the area you're visiting.

A few tips

  • Add your flight details in Turo so we can adjust for delays.
  • Booking last-minute? You can still cancel free up to 4 hours after booking on same-day trips — see our cancellation policy.
  • Traveling with family? Reserve an SUV early; they go fast in season.
