Touring Southwest Florida: Your Ultimate Home-Hunting Road Trip Guide

Thinking about relocating to the Gulf Coast? The best way to understand Southwest Florida is to drive it. Flying into PGD or RSW and renting a car lets you tour neighborhoods on your own schedule — no rideshare surge pricing, no waiting. Here's a route that covers the highlights in a long weekend.

Day 1 — Cape Coral & Fort Myers

Start in Cape Coral, known for its canal-front homes and quiet, family-friendly streets. Cross the bridge into Fort Myers for the historic River District, McGregor Boulevard's royal palms, and easy beach access at Fort Myers Beach and Sanibel.

Day 2 — Punta Gorda & Port Charlotte

Head north to Punta Gorda, a walkable waterfront town with a growing food scene and harbor views, then explore Port Charlotte for more space and value. The drive up US-41 and I-75 is quick and scenic.

Day 3 — Estero, Bonita Springs & Naples

Finish to the south through Estero and Bonita Springs — both popular with new arrivals — and into Naples for upscale shopping and some of the best beaches in the state.

Tips for the trip

  • Build in buffer time — open houses and traffic on US-41 can run long.
  • Pick an SUV if you're touring with family or an agent; a sedan is plenty for solo scouting.
