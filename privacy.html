Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SafeWheels Rentals SWFL, LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide — name, email, phone — when you inquire or book.
  • Reservation information — trip dates, vehicle selected, and pickup/return details.
  • Verification information processed through our booking and identity partners (e.g., Turo, Canopy Connect) such as driver's license and insurance details.
  • Website usage data — basic analytics like pages visited, collected automatically.

How we use it

  • To process and manage your reservation and rental.
  • To communicate with you about bookings, changes, and support.
  • To verify identity and eligibility, and to handle any incidents or claims.
  • To improve our services and comply with legal obligations.

How we share it

We share information only as needed to operate the business — for example with our booking platform (Turo), payment and verification providers, insurers, and, when required, law enforcement. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention & security

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes above and to meet legal requirements, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You can also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

Third-party platforms

Bookings made through Turo are also governed by Turo's own privacy policy. Please review their terms when booking through their platform.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at safewheelsrentals@gmail.com or (657) 432-1456.
