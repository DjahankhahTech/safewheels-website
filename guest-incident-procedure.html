Guest Incident Procedure

What to do during a traffic stop or accident — stay safe, document thoroughly, and notify us promptly.

🚨 If anyone is injured, call 911 immediately. Your safety always comes first.

1. Safety & initial response

  • Pull over to a secure, well-lit location as soon as it's safe.
  • Turn on your hazard lights.
  • Call emergency services if there are any injuries.

2. Police interaction (traffic stop)

Remain calm and be ready to provide:

  • Your driver's license.
  • Vehicle registration (in the glove compartment).
  • Proof of insurance card (in the glove compartment).

Note the officer's name and badge number for your records.

3. Accident documentation

  • Contact 911 if required.
  • Exchange details with any other parties involved.
  • Photograph vehicle damage, VINs, and odometer readings.
  • Capture a screenshot or photo of your booking confirmation and your Canopy Connect verification page.

4. Incident reporting

Contact SafeWheels as soon as possible via our 24/7 hotline: +1 (657) 432-1456 or email safewheelsrentals@gmail.com. Include your reservation number and all incident documentation.

Prompt notification and thorough documentation help us resolve any claim fairly and quickly.
