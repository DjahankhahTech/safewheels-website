Disconnect from QuickBooks

Remove the link between SafeWheels Rentals SWFL and your QuickBooks Online account.

You can disconnect the SafeWheels Rentals SWFL accounting integration from your QuickBooks Online company at any time. Disconnecting revokes our access and stops any further data syncing.

Disconnect from QuickBooks

What happens when you disconnect

  • Your QuickBooks authorization tokens are revoked and our access ends immediately.
  • No further accounting data is read from or written to your QuickBooks company.
  • You can reconnect anytime from the connect page.

Disconnecting from within QuickBooks

You can also remove the connection directly in QuickBooks: go to Settings → Apps → Connected apps, find SafeWheels Rentals SWFL, and choose Disconnect.
