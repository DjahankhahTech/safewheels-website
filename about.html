About SafeWheels Rentals SWFL

A family business that combines a love for automobiles with top-notch customer service.

Hi, I'm Dr. Alex Djahankhah of the Djahankhah Family! Our family business rents out quality cars, combining our love for automobiles with top-notch customer service. As both host and traveler, I value reliability, transparency, and comfort — ensuring every guest enjoys a seamless experience.

Working with us

  • Responsible ownership. I carefully service and maintain each car, keeping them safe, clean, and ready to hit the road at a moment's notice.
  • Transparent communication. Clear, prompt communication keeps all parties informed throughout the rental period.
  • Respect for others. I treat each rental — my own or someone else's — as if it were my own prized possession.
  • Parking reimbursement. We reimburse airport parking costs upon receipt of photographic proof.

A favorite travel experience

Cruising along Southwest Florida's Gulf Coast is always memorable — pristine beaches, palm-lined roads, and endless sunshine make for an adventurous atmosphere.

Beyond the business

When I'm not working, I enjoy photography, hiking, visiting local coffee shops, and keeping up with the latest in technology. My dream car? The Lamborghini Aventador, for its design and performance. On the road, I'm a cautious driver who prioritizes safety and attentiveness in every condition.
